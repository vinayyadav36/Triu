1. Data Controller

EmporiumVipani (operated by Vipani Commerce Pvt. Ltd., India) is the Data Fiduciary under the Digital Personal Data Protection (DPDP) Act 2023. Contact: privacy@emporiumvipani.com

3. Your Rights under DPDP Act 2023

  • Right to Access — Request a copy of all data we hold about you.
  • Right to Correction — Update inaccurate personal information from your profile dashboard.
  • Right to Erasure — Delete your account and all personal data instantly via Profile → Settings → Delete Account. Transactional records are retained for 7 years as mandated by the Income Tax Act.
  • Right to Grievance — Lodge a complaint with our Data Protection Officer within 7 business days.

4. Consent

We collect consent separately for (a) order fulfilment (mandatory) and (b) marketing communications & personalisation (optional). You can withdraw optional consent at any time via your profile. No boxes are pre-ticked.

5. Data Storage & Security

All personal data is stored on encrypted servers in the AWS Mumbai (ap-south-1) region, ensuring data localisation as required under the DPDP Act. We use TLS 1.3 for all data in transit and AES-256 for data at rest.

6. Cookies

We use only functional cookies necessary for authentication (session tokens) and no third-party tracking cookies. Consent for analytics cookies is requested separately via the banner.

7. Grievance Officer

As required by the Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020:

  • Name: Grievance Officer, EmporiumVipani
  • Email: grievance@emporiumvipani.com
  • Response time: 48 hours. Resolution time: 15 working days.

8. Changes to this Policy

We will notify registered users via email at least 30 days before any material change. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
