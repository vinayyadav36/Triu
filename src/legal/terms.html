Terms of Service

Effective: 1 April 2026 · Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020 · CGST Act Section 52

1. Platform Overview

EmporiumVipani (operated by Vipani Commerce Pvt. Ltd., registered in India) is an online marketplace that connects verified third-party sellers ("Sellers") with customers ("Buyers"). EmporiumVipani is an intermediary under the Information Technology Act 2000 and is not the seller of record for third-party listings.

2. Seller Accountability

  • All Sellers are verified via valid GSTIN / PAN before listing products.
  • Sellers must display their legal business name, registered address, GSTIN, and country of origin on their storefront, as required by Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020.
  • All prices shown are inclusive of all applicable taxes (GST). The MRP displayed is the Maximum Retail Price inclusive of GST.
  • Sellers must honour the warranty / return policies displayed at the time of purchase.
  • Sellers must appoint a Grievance Officer whose contact details are published on their store page.

3. Tax & Financial Terms

  • TCS (Section 52, CGST Act): EmporiumVipani, as an e-commerce operator, deducts 1% Tax Collected at Source from each seller payout and remits it to the government.
  • GST invoices with HSN/SAC codes, CGST/SGST (intra-state) or IGST (inter-state) breakdowns, and Place of Supply are issued for every transaction.
  • Order records are retained for 7 years in compliance with the Income Tax Act.

4. Buyer Rights & Returns

  • You have a 7-day return window from delivery for most products (exceptions listed on individual product pages).
  • Refunds are processed within 5–15 working days of return receipt, directly to your original payment method.
  • Defective or misrepresented products are eligible for a full refund including shipping.
  • Flash sales that disadvantage consumers are prohibited on this platform per Consumer Protection Rules 2020.

5. Payment Security

All payments are processed via Razorpay, which is compliant with RBI 2026 guidelines including Two-Factor Authentication (2FA) and tokenised card storage. EmporiumVipani does not store raw card data.

6. Prohibited Activities

  • Listing counterfeit, prohibited, or hazardous goods.
  • Manipulating reviews or ratings.
  • Using the platform for money laundering or tax evasion.
  • Predatory "Flash Sales" designed to harm competition or deceive consumers.

7. Grievance Redressal

As mandated by Consumer Protection (E-Commerce) Rules 2020:

  • Email: grievance@emporiumvipani.com
  • All complaints acknowledged within 48 hours.
  • All complaints resolved within 15 working days.
  • Unresolved disputes may be escalated to the National Consumer Disputes Redressal Commission (NCDRC) or the Online Dispute Resolution (ODR) portal.

8. Governing Law

These terms are governed by Indian law. Disputes shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of courts in India. These terms are subject to change; material updates will be communicated 30 days in advance.
